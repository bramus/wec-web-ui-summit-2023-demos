How to take measurements

  • Bust: Stand with your arms relaxed at your sides. Place the measuring tape under your arms, at the fullest part of your bust. Keep the tape level across your shoulder blades.
  • Natural Waist: Stand up straight and find the natural crease of your waist. Place the measuring tape around your waist, keeping the tape comfortably loose.
  • Drop Waist: Measure around your body 1 1/2 inches below your natural waist.
  • Hips: Stand with your feet together. Place the measuring tape around the fullest part of your hips
